About Our Organization

ΔΣΠ is one of the largest co-ed professional business fraternities in the United States. It was founded over 100 years ago at New York University and has initiated over 275,000 members. Sigma Omega is Northeasten Univerty Delta Sigma Pi Chapter. We were founded on November 14, 2014 and have initiated over 100 of our own brothers. We may be a newer chapter, but our growth is stronger than ever.
